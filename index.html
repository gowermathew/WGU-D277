Alaska, The Last Frontier

Juneau | Anchorage | Ketchikan |

Quick Facts

  • Purchased from Russia in 1867 for 7.2 million dollars.
  • The largest state in the U.S. by area.
  • 3197 officially named lakes

Geography

Alaska is the largest U.S. state, spanning across 663000 square miles! That's larger than the next three largest states combined. Alaska also has more coastline than all other states combined.

Population

As of 2024 Alaska has a population around 740000. That only adds up to about 1 person per square mile, making it the most sparsely populated state in the nation.

Natural Wonders

Alaska is home to some of the most stunning natural wonders in the world, including Denali, the highest peak in North America, and the breathtaking fjords of Kenai Fjords National Park. The state also boasts over 3,000 officially named lakes, including Lake Iliamna, the largest lake in Alaska and the third largest in the United States.
